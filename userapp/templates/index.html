Have A Questions?

1 What kind of cases fall under civil law?
Cases involving contracts, property, personal injury, family issues, and disputes over rights.
2 What is the outcome of a civil case?
Civil cases typically result in compensation, restitution, or other remedies rather than criminal penalties.
3 What is legal ethics?
Legal ethics refers to the standards of professional conduct that lawyers must follow, ensuring integrity and fairness in their practice.
4 What is the purpose of a trial?
The purpose of a trial is to resolve legal disputes by presenting evidence and arguments before a judge or jury for a decision.
5 What is a contract in legal terms?
A contract is a legally binding agreement between two or more parties that outlines their rights and obligations.
